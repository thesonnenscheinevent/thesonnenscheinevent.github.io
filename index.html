Welcome to Sonnenschein

Bringing Your Dream Events to Life

"Sonnenschein," German for sunshine, is the core of our philosophy. We bring warmth, brilliance, and meticulous planning to every event, turning your special moments into radiant, lifelong memories.

Based in the heart of West Bengal, we are a full-service event management company specializing in bespoke weddings, sophisticated corporate events, and vibrant social gatherings. Our passionate team is dedicated to understanding your vision and executing it flawlessly.

With a comprehensive approach that covers everything from creative design to logistical precision, we ensure every detail is perfect, allowing you to relax and enjoy your celebration. Let us add a touch of sunshine to your next event!
